I am an interdisciplinary scientist working across geospatial science, ecology, and conservation biology in the Science Innovation Area at UNEP-WCMC. I'm also a PhD student in the Plant Production Systems chair group at Wageningen University.

My work currently focuses on the impacts and dependencies that cocoa farming has on nature, building models based on fieldwork around West and Central Africa.

I sometimes also work on the science-policy interface, researching how we are making progress (or not!) towards our global goals for nature.
